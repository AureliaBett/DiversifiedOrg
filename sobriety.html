Our Mission

To provide a safety net for queer youth in Kenya, offering economic empowerment, mental health resources, harm reduction, and community care to help them build mental fortitude and navigate their identities.

We advocate for queer rights,
emphasizing the need for funding
and financial empowerment in the community

Our Vission

Empower queer youth to thrive, reclaim their stories, and build sustainable lives through resilience and community support.
