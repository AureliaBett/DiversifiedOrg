Queer Mass

Every year we at Diversified Kenya step into our santa boots, ready to sprinkle some queer magic over the festive season! We pride ourselves on reclaiming and queering christmass, turning it into affirming, yassified and joyfull celebration for our vibrant LGBTQIA+ community. We know how tricky this time of the year can be for many of us, but thats why we are here- to create a joyfull family we all deserve,
From hosting some of the most inclusive, affirming, queer spaces, running a Sobriety Clinic, and building a whatsapp community of 300+ LGBTQIA+ folx, to amplifying queer voices through art, love, and resistance- Diversified Kenya is all about community care. Every December 25th, we honour that by hostig queermass: a family style, blood tie free celebratio of joy, acceptance, and collective care.
Imagine this:

  • An all-you-can-eat spread filled with the best Kenyan festive cuisine
  • A space to dance, cook, vibe, and celebrate together.
  • A sober but welcoming atmosphere where you can bring your drinks if you'd like.
  • A place where queer joy reigns supreme and laughter feels like home
