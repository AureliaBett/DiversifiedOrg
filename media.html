Queer Mass

It is that time of the year when Diversified kenya steps into our santa boots, ready to sprinkle some queer magic over the festive season! We pride ourselves on reclaiming and queering christmass, turning it into affirming, yassified and joyfull celebration for our vibrant LGBTQIA+ community. We know how tricky this time of the year can be for many of us, but thats why we are here- to create a joyfull family we all deserve,

Outdoor Play Date

Kanunga Falls 200 Queer Hike

On a bright and energizing day, we organized a successful queer hike, bringing together 200 vibrant individuals to explore the breathtaking Kanunga Falls in Murang'a County. The event was filled with laughter, camaraderie, and the shared joy of connecting with nature. Hikers marveled at the serene beauty of the falls, with cascading waters creating the perfect backdrop for relaxation and memorable photos. From challenging trails to moments of reflection by the water, everyone found something to treasure. The sense of community and belonging was palpable, making it not just a hike but a celebration of authenticity, adventure, and togetherness. A day well spent, with memories to last a lifetime!
