About Us

Sobriety Clinic

A communal initiative offering support for substance abuse recovery, supplemented by professional mental health services in collaboration with Mental 360.

Mental wellness Through Art

We provide therapy and creative workshops to 200 participants yearly, supporting trauma healing and personal growth. These workshops use art for emotional healing and self expression

Economic Empowerment

Woodwork workshop, financial literacy, career development, and entrepreneurship support, with peer-led skill swaps for crafts like soap-making, wig-making, and online business management.

About Us

Everyone deserves a circle of inclusion and influence, but it is up to you to create it.

Welcome to Diversified Kenya, an LBQ organization dedicated to empowering, educating, and sensitizing the community. Our misssion is to provide resources and to support those who need it most.

We create a safe and inclusive enviroment where everyone is welcome. We believe in creating awareness about the unique challanges that the LBQ community faces, and we are commited to bridging the gap between community and society as a whole.
